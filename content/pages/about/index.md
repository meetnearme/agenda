## About Us

We're passionate about connecting people with the best local events happening in their community.

Every week, we curate a list of the most exciting events - from live music and food festivals to art shows and community gatherings. Our goal is simple: help you discover what's happening and never miss out on the good stuff.

### Our Mission

To be your trusted source for local event discovery, delivered in a simple, no-spam weekly newsletter.

### Get In Touch

Have an event you'd like us to feature? Reach out and let us know!
