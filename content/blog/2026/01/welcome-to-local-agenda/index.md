Welcome to your new newsletter-first publication!

## What's Next?

This theme is designed to help you share weekly event guides with your community. Here's how to get started:

1. **Customize your branding** - Update the site title and navigation in the CMS
2. **Add your first real post** - Share what's happening in your area this week
3. **Connect your newsletter** - Integrate Beehiiv to start collecting subscribers
4. **Spread the word** - Share your publication on social media

## Features

- Beautiful dark theme with lime green accents
- Newsletter-first design with prominent signup forms
- Blog/posts system with featured images
- CMS-powered content management with Decap CMS
- Fast and SEO-friendly with Next.js

Get started by heading to `/admin` to access the CMS and create your first real post!
